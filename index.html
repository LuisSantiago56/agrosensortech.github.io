AgroSensor Technologies

Welcome to AgroSensor Technologies. This is an Embedded Design project that tries to take advantages of technology into agriculture. Allowing you to have an independent watering system that measure the soil moisture and decide when to water your crops. Mantain this way the soil moisture of your garden far from lack of watering and or over watering. Also it gives you reports of the soil moisture versus time through the day and the exact time when the system watered the corps and the time it took.
